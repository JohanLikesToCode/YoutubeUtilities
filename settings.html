YouTube Utilities

Extension Information

About this Extension

YouTube Utilities enhances your YouTube experience by allowing you to customize playback settings, remove ads, and improve control over your viewing experience. Enjoy an ad-free experience with additional options!

Features:

  • Ad Removal BETA: Skip or block most YouTube ads seamlessly.
  • Thanks Donate Removal: No more giving money to youtubers. Youtube does that.
  • Premium Button Remover: Get rid of intrusive Premium-only buttons such as Download/ Clip

How to Use:

There isn't anything to do! All is automated.
